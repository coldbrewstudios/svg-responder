Responsive SVG Maker

What Does R-SVG do?

R-SVG is a tool that makes SVG's responsive. Take SVGS from anywhere: Sketch, Photoshop, Invision etc and convert them to responsive auto-scaling SVG's!

R-SVG does not upload files to a server, we will never see, share or store your files.

What do I have to do?

Upload a SVG (or multiple), once the convertion has happened, click the SVG thumbnail to save it.
